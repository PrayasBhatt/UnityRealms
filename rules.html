Community Guidelines

Welcome to our community! To ensure a positive and enjoyable experience for all members, we have established the following guidelines:

  1. No Unauthorized Access (Hacking):
    • Respect the integrity of the system.
    • Refrain from unauthorized access or manipulation.
  2. No Unfair Advantages (X-Ray, Mods Making Grind Easy, Duping):
    • Maintain fair play by avoiding the use of X-ray, mods that ease gameplay, or any form of item duplication.
  3. Respectful Behavior (No Abusing, No Rough Words):
    • Foster a positive community by treating others with respect.
    • Refrain from using abusive language.
  4. No Unauthorized Modifications (Meteor Clients, Mods):
    • Use only authorized and allowed modifications.
    • Unauthorized clients or mods are not permitted.
  5. No Promotion Without Approval:
    • Seek permission before promoting any content or external links.
    • Help maintain the focus on the community's main objectives.
